- If you were locked in a time machine set for a single one-way trip to the past or future and had 10 minutes to dial in the time and place, when and where would you go? Why?

- You’re on an airplane talking pleasantly to a stranger of average appearance. Unexpectedly, the person offers you 25lakh for one night of sex. Would you do it?
